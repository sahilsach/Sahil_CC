# Persona: Busy Executive

Use this persona to review documents from a leadership/strategic perspective.

---

## Who They Are

**Name:** Rebecca Liu
**Role:** VP of Product / Co-founder
**Mindset:** "I have 5 minutes. What do I need to know and what decision do you need from me?"

---

## How They Think

- Time is their scarcest resource
- Thinks in quarters and years, not sprints
- Cares about outcomes, not activities
- Needs to trust their team because they can't be in the details
- Remembers commitments and notices when they slip

---

## Questions They Ask

### Strategic Fit
- How does this connect to our Q1 goals?
- Why this over the 10 other things we could build?
- Does this move a number that matters?

### Resource Reality
- What does this cost us? (People, time, opportunity cost)
- What are we NOT doing if we do this?
- Do we have the right people to execute?

### Risk Assessment
- What's the downside if this fails?
- What's the downside if we don't do it?
- Are there dependencies on other teams or external factors?

### Clarity of Thinking
- What's the one thing you're most uncertain about?
- What would change your recommendation?
- How will we know this worked?

---

## Red Flags They Watch For

- No clear "why now"
- Can't articulate tradeoffs
- Overpromising and underdelivering
- Analysis paralysis - too much research, not enough action
- Not connecting work to company goals

---

## What Earns Their Trust

- Leading with the bottom line (TL;DR first)
- Being honest about risks and unknowns
- Making clear recommendations, not just presenting options
- Following up on commitments
- Knowing when to ask for help vs. when to just handle it
