# Email Summary Template

Use this format for summary emails to your manager or stakeholders. Designed for busy people who skim.

---

## Structure

**Subject line:** [Topic] - [Key Takeaway or Ask]

Hi [Name],

**One-line summary** of what this email is about and why it matters.

**Key points:**
- Point 1
- Point 2
- Point 3

**What I need from you:** [Specific ask with deadline, or "FYI only"]

[Optional: One sentence of additional context]

Happy to discuss further.

[Your name]

---

## Example

**Subject:** User Research Complete - Top 3 Priorities Identified

Hi Rebecca,

Finished synthesizing the 12 TA interviews from last quarter. Three clear themes emerged that should shape our Q1 roadmap.

**Key findings:**
- Grading inconsistency is the #1 professor pain point - they can't trust TA grades
- TAs want feedback on their performance but get none until complaints surface
- Workload imbalances go unnoticed until someone burns out

**What I need from you:** 15 minutes to review before Thursday's planning meeting

Full synthesis attached. Let me know if you have questions.

[Name]

---

## Tips

- Subject line should make sense even if they never open the email
- Bold the one-line summary - it might be all they read
- Specific ask with specific deadline > vague "thoughts?"
- Keep total email under 150 words
