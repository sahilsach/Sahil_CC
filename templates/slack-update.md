# Slack Update Template

Use this format for team updates posted to Slack channels. Keep it brief and scannable.

---

## Structure

**Emoji + Bold headline** (1 line)

Quick context line (optional)

**Key points:**
- Point 1
- Point 2
- Point 3

**Action needed:** [if any]

Link to full doc: [if relevant]

---

## Example

:memo: **User Research Synthesis Complete**

Finished analyzing 12 interviews from last quarter's TA research.

**Top findings:**
- Grading consistency is the #1 pain point for professors
- TAs feel isolated - no feedback on their grading quality
- Workload distribution is invisible until things break

**Action needed:** Review and add comments by EOD Friday

Full doc: [link]

---

## Tips

- Lead with an emoji that signals the type of update
- Keep to 5-7 lines maximum
- One clear ask if action is needed
- Always link to the full document for details
